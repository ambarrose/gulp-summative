Hotel

A haven for adventurous travelers since 1884, the world-renowned Hermitage Hotel is nestled among stunning scenery within the Aoraki Mount Cook National Park, offering a complete alpine holiday experience for all.

About
Accommodation ranges from luxurious hotel rooms with unsurpassed views of Aoraki Mt Cook to standard rooms overlooking the Sefton mountain range. Several Restaurants and bars offer an assortment of dining options with spectacular views from every seat.

Experience world renowned activities, including a cruise amongst glaciers, explore the Tasman valley, stargaze within the International Dark Sky Reserve or be educated and entertained in the Sir Edmund Hillary Alpine Centre.

Facilities and Features
Breakfast, Children Welcome, Conference Facilities, Dinner, Disabled Access, Laundry, Licensed Bar, Lift, Lunch, Restaurant.

Hostel

Located in the heart of the city of Wellington,The Marion Hostel offers a range of backpacker accommodation and features a rooftop terrace.

It is just a 1-minute walk from Cuba Street where you will find a variety of restaurants, cafes and bars. Free high speed unlimited WiFi is offered throughout the property.

The property offers a choice of rooms including spacious dormitory rooms and private rooms, including one private room with an en suite. All rooms offer complimentary towels, hairdryer and toiletries. The dormitory rooms include reading lights, USB plugs, privacy curtains and lockers. Linen is provided for all rooms.

Guests have access to shared fully equipped kitchens and dining area as well as two spacious guest lounge areas with large TV’s with Netflix. The Marion Hostel is within a 5-minute walk from Wellington's vibrant nightlife on Courtney Place and the Te Papa Museum.

Motel

Regal Residency offers accommodation with a garden and free WiFi throughout the property as well as free private parking for guests who drive.

Featuring family rooms, this property also provides guests with a sun terrace. Local points of interest like ASB Showgrounds and Auckland War Memorial Museum are reachable within 7 km and 7 km, respectively.

The motel comes with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries and a shower. The units will provide guests with a wardrobe and a kettle.

The area is popular for cycling, and bike hire is available at Regal Residency.

House

Our newly renovated Beach Bach studio is a stand-alone, private, clean, self-contained. Comfortable bed, wardrobe, kitchen, dining table, air conditioning, bathroom, shower, toilet. All linen provided. Features covered outdoor entertaining area, BBQ & outdoor furniture. Close to restaurants, shops and The Lost Springs.

The studio is a private space which is separate from our house. The studio has its own access through the gate at the front of the property next to the large shed. The studio is perfect for a couple getaway.

There is also a kitchenette with tea and coffee making facilities, microwave, electric fry pan, fridge, kettle and toaster. Tea and coffee provided.

Enjoy your surroundings with a relaxing drink in the patio area, cook on the BBQ relax and enjoy the laid back kiwi way in our beach studio - don’t forget your beach towels (not provided).
